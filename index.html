What is Reported Speech?

Reported speech (also called indirect speech) is when we tell someone what another person said without using their exact words.

Key Rules to Remember:

  • Tense Changes: When reporting speech, we usually change the tense
  • Pronoun Changes: Pronouns may need to change based on context
  • Time/Place Changes: Words like "today", "here" may change to "that day", "there"

Tense Changes in Reported Speech
